Elena Moore

Creative developer & visual storyteller

Hey there! I'm Elena — a designer, photographer, and curious mind based between Seattle and Berlin. I craft digital experiences that feel intuitive and celebrate honest design. This portfolio is a living archive of my journey, from experimental web projects to documentary photography and professional certifications.

With over 6 years in the creative industry, I've collaborated with studios, non-profits, and independent artists. I believe in thoughtful minimalism, inclusive design, and the magic of human connection. Outside work, I capture fleeting moments on film, read sci-fi, and drink way too much matcha.
